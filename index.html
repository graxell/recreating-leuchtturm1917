The new premium 129G Edition. A matter of transparency

The new 120G Edition notebooks are a perfect choice for anyone who loves to combine writing with sketching and drawing. By adding the right filler, the paper has a very low transparency. Writing and drawings are not visible from the back.

Whether you choose a classic notebook, bullet journal or use the paper for hand lettering: The properties of this special paper mean that ut us suitable for a whole host of uses. The 120G Edition. A matter of transparency. Page for page - pleasure that unfolds.

For an extra fine ink stroke - Drehgriffel Nr. 1 with Gel Ink

Inspired by the Asian art of writing with its filigree typeface made up of detailed characters, the award-winning design of the Drehgriffel Nr. 1 pen now comes with a Japanese 0.5mm gel pen refill.

The gel ink used has a significantly higher viscosity than the ink in a ballpoint pen, and the ink flows evenly from the pen tip onto the paper. The result is smooth writing with fine contours.

Bullet Journal® - Edition 2. The new, improved original.

In cooperation with the international Bullet Journaling community and designer Ryder Carroll, we are writing the next successful chapter of the original Bullet Journal®.

The intensive exchange with the BuJo community has generated ideas for new features and details, with which Ryder Carroll's ground-breaking method for self-organisation can be integrated into your own life even more intuitively. Enjoy more individual structure and creativity - with the new Bullet Journal® - Edition 2.

  • Specially developed low-transparency 129 g/m2 paper
  • Page Dividers for smart markings on each paper G
  • rid Guide for calendar, overviews, diagrams and trackers
  • Sticker Set with practical monthly and weekly overviews
  • Pocket Guide with Bullet Journaling instructions
